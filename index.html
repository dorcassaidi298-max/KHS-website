Wall Skimming FAQ

Wall skimming is the application of a thin layer of finishing plaster over existing walls to create a perfectly flat and smooth surface. It's ideal for walls that are uneven, textured, or have minor cracks.
We recommend waiting at least 5–7 days for the skim coat to fully cure before painting, depending on humidity and temperature. We'll advise on the right timing for your specific conditions.
